Decadent Three-Cheese Bacon Potatoes Au Gratin

creamy cheesy potatoes with lardon la bacon!

This creamy, decadent dish has an overall mellow flavor but with sparks of tangy blue cheese and smoky bacon. Truly the perfect side dish for a special occasion meal.

Sear the top for a crispy crunchy cheese experience. fun for the whole family.


Prep: 20 mins.
Serves 8
Cook: 1hr 20 mins
Total: 1hr 50 mins

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • more cheese
  • damn, more cheese
  • cooking spray or oil
  • 4 slices smoked bacon, chopped, or more to taste
  • 6 medium russet potatoes, thinkly sliced
  • 1 medium sweet yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 pinch garlic powder to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup Gruyere
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups Heavy Cream
  • 2 tablestpoons butter, cut into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until almost crispy, 6 to 8 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels.
  3. Wash potatoes and slice into ⅛" thin slices. Slice onion as thin as possible.
  4. Layer potatoes and onions in little stacks. Place stacks upright in a greased casserole 2.5qt to 3qt dish.
  5. Melt butter & flour in a saucepan and cook 2-3 minutes. Add seasonings and milk. Whisk over medium heat until thick and bubbly. Remove from heat and stir in cheeses until melted.
  6. Spoon cheese sauce over potatoes. Cover with foil (sprayed with cooking spray) and bake 60 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake an additional 20-30 minutes or until lightly browned and potatoes are cooked. Cool 15 minutes before serving.
